Ritmo Latino Fest: Site Plan

Author: Adelso Tzep

Site Name

Ritmo Latino Fest

Reason: This name was selected because it is punchy, easy to remember, and clearly communicates the focus of the festival (Latin Rhythms). It sounds vibrant and inviting for a music-centered event.

Optional domain: ritmolatinofest.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as the digital home for the Ritmo Latino Fest. It provides fans with the official artist lineup, event scheduling, and a way to sign up for news updates. Its goal is to build excitement and provide essential logistics for attendees.

Scenarios

  • Who are the headlining artists this year and what time do they perform?
  • How can I sign up to receive notifications about ticket sales and future announcements?

Color Scheme

The selected color scheme is designed to evoke the passion and energy of Latin music:

  • Primary Color: #AB001A (Crimson Red) - Used for the header background, main headings, and primary action buttons to represent energy.
  • Secondary Color: #333333 (Dark Charcoal) - Used for body text and footer background to provide strong readability and a modern feel.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Montserrat', sans-serif - A bold, modern font used for all H1 and H2 tags to grab attention.

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - A clean, highly readable font used for all paragraphs and list items.
